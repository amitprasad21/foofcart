Our Story

Elevating the online dining experience in Greater Noida.

A Vision for Better Food Delivery

FoodCart was born out of a simple observation: ordering food online shouldn't mean sacrificing restaurant-quality standards. We recognized a gap in the Greater Noida market for a brand that treats cloud kitchen operations with the same culinary respect as a fine-dining establishment.

We operate an advanced, hygiene-first cloud kitchen situated primarily in Knowledge Park 3. By cutting out the middle-men dining room costs, we shift all our resources into sourcing incredible ingredients, hiring phenomenal chefs, and ensuring lightning-fast delivery.

Our WhatsApp-first approach allows us to directly connect with our customers, offering a personalized, seamless ordering experience without the clutter of aggregator apps.
